Structuring Document

Header (header) Element:

Header is the Introductory content of a webpage which can be used to indicate the introduction of the webpage. It can have Navigation Pane, Logo of the webpage etcetra

Navigation (nav) Element:

Navigation element is the place where the contents will be navigated other pages in the web page or website. And not all the links must be in nav element.

Main (main) Element:

It is the key and unique content of the webpage or website. It is bounrded inside the body tag. It cannot be a descendant of article, aside, header, footer, and nav element.
